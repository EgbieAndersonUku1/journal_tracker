Job Tracker

JobJournalTracker is a web based journal application that allows you the user to consolidate all your job searches, job applications, interviews, responses from employers into one easy to view place.

Different sites like Indeed, Linkedln, etc allow you to keep a record of your job history but not if the job was applied using a different job site. For example you won't be able to see your indeed job history in your Linkedln account and if you applied for other jobs using any other sites you certainly won't be able to see them in any of your job accounts, not to mention keeping track off all those job searches from different accounts can be a hassle

Once you have created an account, JobJournalTracker allows to keep track off all the job you applied for using an easy to use user interface. For example, once you applied for a job using whatever job site you like, simple log into JobJournalTracker and add that job to your account including details like the title, the description, the company, the location, employment contract e.g. Zero hour, contract, full time, part-time, etc,

You can also add a journal along your recorded job describing what you are doing or done in preparation for the job, the url for the job, etc. JobJournalTracker also allows you to edit, delete, search or view any jobs that you have added to the site. You can easily search for your jobs using location, employment contract, title and so much more

Whether you are a freelancer, agency worker, contractor, jobseeker, etc keeping track off all your jobs has never been easier. No more using software like Word, Excel, etc to keep track off all your job sites, responses, now you can simply do it here
